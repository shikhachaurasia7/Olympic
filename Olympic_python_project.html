Conclusion:

Michael Phelps is the most decorated athlete, with the USA leading in total medals, especially in swimming (26.5%). The year 2008 saw the highest number of swimming medals. Athletes aged 30-40 dominated in equestrian and curling. In terms of medal distribution, swimming accounted for 26.5%, athletics for 26.1%, and rowing for 19.9%.

Recommendation

To improve performance in the Olympics, countries should invest in swimming and athletics, as they contribute the highest medal counts. Training programs should focus on athletes aged 30-40, especially in equestrian and curling, where experience plays a key role. Identifying and nurturing young talent early can help nations compete at the highest level. Additionally, analyzing past trends can guide better resource allocation for future events.
